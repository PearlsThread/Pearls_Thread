Our story

From a single skein to a full bouquet

Pearls Thread began the way most good things in wool do — with one hook, one ball of yarn, and a lot of patience. What started as a handful of flowers made for fun has grown into a small collection of bouquets, clips and keepsakes, each one still looped by hand, stitch by stitch.

There's no machine involved anywhere in the process. Every petal is shaped, every bow is tied, and every bouquet is wrapped to order — which means your piece is made only after you ask for it, in the colours you choose.

Caring for your wool blooms

Keep pieces away from direct sun and damp spots, give them a gentle shake if the petals get flattened in transit, and store loose in a drawer rather than under weight. Handled gently, a Pearls Thread piece easily outlasts a real bouquet.
